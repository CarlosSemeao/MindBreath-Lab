User Privacy Choices

Control how MindBreath handles your data. We keep things simple: no accounts, no third-party ads, and your session history is stored on your device.

Last updated: September 9, 2025

1) Export or Delete Your Data

MindBreath stores your breathing session counts locally (on-device). You can:

  • Export CSV from the Progress tab (tap “Export CSV”).
  • Delete data by uninstalling the app, or email us and we’ll guide you to clear the app’s local storage.

Email data request

CSV export copies data to your clipboard so you can paste into Notes, Numbers, or Excel.

2) Notifications

MindBreath may request notification permission (if you enable reminders in the future).

  • iOS: Settings → Notifications → MindBreath → Allow/Disallow.
  • Android: Settings → Apps → MindBreath → Notifications.

3) Analytics & Tracking

MindBreath does not use third-party analytics SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

  • No ad networks, no cross-app tracking.
  • No login or account required.

4) Clipboard Usage (CSV Export)

When you export history, MindBreath places a CSV string on your device clipboard. Nothing is sent to our servers.

  • You can clear the clipboard anytime (e.g., copy any other text).

5) Platform Privacy Controls

Manage broader platform settings:

  • Apple: App privacy settings
  • Android: Privacy controls
