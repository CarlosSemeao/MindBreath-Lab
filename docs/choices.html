User Privacy Choices

MindBreath is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, share, or sell any personal data.

Your Choices

  • Data Storage: All session history is stored locally on your device only.
  • No Accounts: MindBreath does not require you to create an account or log in.
  • No Tracking: We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics.
  • Delete Anytime: You may delete the app at any time to remove all data from your device.
