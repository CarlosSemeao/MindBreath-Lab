Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-09-09

Our promise

MindBreath is designed to be calm and private. We don’t sell your data, we don’t track you across apps, and your breathing history stays on your device unless you choose to export it.

Data we store

  • Breathing sessions & settings — stored locally on your device.
  • Crash diagnostics (optional) — platform-provided, anonymized diagnostics if you opt in to share with developers.

Data we don’t collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, contacts).
  • No location data.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No ads or advertising trackers.

Exports

If you export your session history (CSV), the file is generated on-device and only shared where you choose (e.g., Notes or Mail).

Children’s privacy

MindBreath is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we’ll help remove it.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email carlos@infrahardener.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Effective” date above will reflect the latest version.
