When reporting an issue, include your device model and OS version if possible.

How do I export my breathing history?

Open Settings → Export CSV. Your session history will be copied to the clipboard so you can paste it into Notes, Mail, or a spreadsheet.

How do I change the theme?

Go to Settings → Appearance and choose Light, Dark, or System.

The breathing cues are too fast/slow.

Try another pattern (e.g., Box, 4-7-8) or create a custom one with your preferred inhale, hold, and exhale counts.

Does MindBreath work offline?

Yes. All features work without an internet connection. Your data stays on your device.

How do I send feedback or a feature request?

We’d love to hear from you — email carlos@infrahardener.com.
